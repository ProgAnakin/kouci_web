Water polo is one of the most demanding team sports in the world — and one of the least served by good software. Coaches still track penalties on paper, draw set plays on whiteboards that get wiped after every session, and rely on memory to tell whether the left-side attack is actually working.

**Kouci changes that.**

## What Kouci does

Kouci turns raw match data into a tactical edge. Instead of scattered notes, you get one place to:

- **Manage your squad** — player profiles, positions, caps, and per-game availability.
- **Map penalties** — every exclusion and 5-metre, plotted by zone, player, and phase of the game.
- **Animate tactics** — build set plays once, replay them, and share them with your staff.
- **Track live matches** — log events as they happen, then review the story afterwards.

It is designed around how water polo is actually coached — not adapted from a generic field-sport tool.

## Built for the pool deck

Every decision in Kouci starts with a simple question: _does this help a coach win the next game?_ The interface is fast, works on the devices you already have, and keeps your data yours.

> Good analysis is not about more numbers. It is about the right number, at the right moment, in a form you can act on.
